A 7-day itinerary across Japan's golden route provides a balanced exploration of Tokyo, Kyoto, and Osaka [1]. Day 1-2 (Tokyo): Begin with the historic Senso-ji Temple and the bustling Shibuya Crossing, followed by a visit to the Meiji Jingu Shrine and the vibrant districts of Shinjuku or Akihabara [1]. Day 3-5 (Kyoto): Travel to Kyoto to experience the iconic Fushimi Inari Shrine, the golden Kinkaku-ji pavilion, and the serene Arashiyama Bamboo Grove [1]. Recent developments highlight the importance of booking these popular cultural sites in advance due to high visitor demand [2]. Day 6-7 (Osaka): Conclude the trip in Osaka by exploring the historic Osaka Castle, followed by an evening of culinary exploration in the Dotonbori district, known for its neon lights and street food culture [1]. This schedule optimizes travel time between cities via the Shinkansen, ensuring a comprehensive experience of Japan's top urban and cultural landmarks [1], [2].
